2. Micro-Services Design
A good design is a modular design. A design solely comprised of a series of micro-services. Each service is tuned to handle simple task. A simple task such as listening for an event on a source system, extracting a simple record from source, simple transformation, or simple preparation of data for analytical use. Most complicated designs can be broken up to such simple parts.

Our micro-services based Data Integration design is:

 Robust: each service can be updated at any time
 Scalable: replica services can be added to scale horizontally
 Decoupled: new features can be implemented with ease
